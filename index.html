Why Use Citation Fetcher?

Everything you need to generate perfect BibTeX citations in seconds.

Smart Automation

Automatically fetches complete citation data including authors, year, journal, and DOI.

Multiple APIs

Uses Crossref, ArXiv, and Semantic Scholar APIs for maximum coverage.

Unicode Support

Privacy First

How To Use

Three simple steps from paper titles to ready-to-use BibTeX.

1

Enter Your Papers

Paste paper titles, DOIs, or arXiv IDs in the input panel. Upload a file or drag and drop.

2

Generate Citations

Click Generate and watch as citations are fetched from Crossref, ArXiv, and Semantic Scholar.

3

Copy & Export

Copy to clipboard, download as BibTeX/RIS/APA/MLA, or share with a link.

Browser Bookmarklet

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any journal page, arXiv abstract, or paper URL to instantly open Citation Fetcher with the paper pre-filled.

Cite This!

Drag the "Cite This!" button to your browser's bookmarks bar.

When browsing a journal page or arXiv abstract, click the bookmarklet.

Citation Fetcher opens with the paper's DOI or title pre-filled.

Powered By Academic APIs

Trusted academic data sources for accurate citations.

Crossref API

Primary

High-quality metadata from thousands of academic publishers.

  • Most accurate citation data
  • Includes DOI links
  • Structured BibTeX output

ArXiv API

Fallback

Open access to preprints in physics, mathematics, computer science, and more.

  • Free and open access
  • Great for recent papers
  • No rate limits

Semantic Scholar

Alternative

AI-powered academic search engine with comprehensive coverage.

  • AI-powered recommendations
  • Citation graphs
  • Abstract analysis

Privacy Policy

No Data Collection

Citation Fetcher runs entirely in your browser. No paper titles or citation data are sent to any server except the official Crossref and ArXiv APIs.

Local Storage

We only store your preferences (like email and delay settings) locally in your browser. You can clear this anytime from settings.
